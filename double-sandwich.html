Double Toast Sandwich

This delicious sandwich can be prepped and eaten in mere seconds!

This delicious little thing sure packs a punch: that's a promise!

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Toaster

Steps:

  1. Slice off five slices of bread.
  2. Place one slice in toaster.
  3. While you wait, call your family over. They don't want to miss this!
  4. Place cooked toast between four slices and enjoy, whilst family watches, jealous of your meal.
