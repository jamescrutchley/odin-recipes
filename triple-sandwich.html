Triple Toast Sandwich

This delicious sandwich can be prepped and eaten in mere seconds!

In the post-war scarcity of the late 40s, my family did what they could to keep their spirits up.

Mostly, we ate sandwiches. This delicious little thing sure packs a punch: that's a promise!

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Toaster

Steps:

  1. Slice off seven slices of bread.
  2. Place one slice in toaster.
  3. While you wait, call your family over. They don't want to miss this!
  4. Place cooked toast between six slices and enjoy, whilst family watches, jealous of your meal.
